Face and Digit Classification (Private)


Basic 2 layer neural network and perceptron coded in Python to perform optical character recognition on face images and handwritten digits with varying data sizes.
Results: In general, larger data sizes result in longer training times, but higher accuracy. Smaller data sizes result in shorter training times, but lower accuracy.
Diminishing returns in accuracy after a certain data size shows that more data points doesn't mean better accuracy and wastes time and resources to train the network.
